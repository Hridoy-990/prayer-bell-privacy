Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Prayer Bell is a free mobile application that provides Islamic prayer times, Qibla direction, and Quran audio. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app uses and why.

Information We Use

Prayer Bell does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All information stays on your device.

Permissions

  • Location – Used to calculate accurate prayer times and Qibla direction for your area. Your location is never sent to our servers or shared with third parties.
  • Notifications – Used to send Adhan alerts at prayer times. You can customise or disable these at any time in Settings.
  • Background Activity – Required to deliver notifications at exact prayer times even when the app is not open.
  • Internet – Used to fetch prayer times and display ads.
  • Vibrate – Used for notification vibration.

Advertisements

Prayer Bell is free and supported by non-personalised banner ads. We do not use your personal data for advertising purposes.

Children's Privacy

Prayer Bell is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
